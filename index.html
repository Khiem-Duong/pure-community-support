The Model

How Every Purchase Helps

1

You Buy a Product

Choose from our collection of Hue traditional handicrafts, each made with care by local artisans.

2

Profit Is Split

After operating costs, 50% of net profit goes to community support and 50% to product development.

3

Community Is Supported

Funds go directly to disadvantaged families in Hue — focused on children's health, living conditions, and education.

The Makers

Made by Hands
Rooted in Hue

Our products are crafted by a network of local artisan partners and our founders' own team. Each piece carries the cultural memory of Hue — passed down through generations of craft.

We credit every artisan on the products they make, and a dedicated section of our site honours all those who have contributed to what Pure has become.
